Beef Qurma

Beef Qorma is a rich, flavorful dish featuring tender pieces of beef slow-cooked in a blend of aromatic spices, yogurt, and onions. Its deep, golden hue comes from the careful balance of spices like turmeric, cumin, and coriander, combined with the subtle warmth of cinnamon and cardamom. The dish is finished with a touch of ghee or oil to create a luscious, creamy texture. Perfectly paired with naan or rice, Beef Qorma is a must-try for anyone craving an indulgent, savory experience. Ideal for a restaurant menu, this dish promises a delightful, authentic taste of traditional South Asian cuisine.

Price: 1000 kg
